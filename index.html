Beam Bending Stress Formula

The central formula for beam design is:

σ = M y / I

This equation returns bending stress using:

  • σ: bending stress in MPa (N/mm²)
  • M: bending moment in N·mm
  • y: distance from the neutral axis in mm
  • I: second moment of area in mm⁴
