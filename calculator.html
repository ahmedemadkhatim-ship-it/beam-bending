Enter values and run the calculation.

Calculation guidance

The moment of inertia is a property of the beam cross-section. The distance y is measured from the neutral axis to the fiber where stress is evaluated.

Recommended units

  • M: N·mm
  • y: mm
  • I: mm⁴
  • σ: MPa

Formula reminder

When using consistent units, the stress formula still follows σ = M y / I. This system converts your values to MPa automatically.
